I am the holder of the Wilfried Guth Endowed Professorship for Constitutional Political Economy and Competition Policy at the University of Freiburg (since 2012). My research focuses on institutions, factor mobility (especially migration), and conflict. I also work on a modern interpretation of Ordnungspolitik in the tradition of the Freiburg School.

For chair information (teaching, staff, organization), please see the official website: uni-freiburg.de/wguth.
